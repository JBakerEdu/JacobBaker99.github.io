Recipe: Italian Sausage Biscuits

Indulge in the savory delight of Italian Sausage Biscuits! These homemade biscuits are bursting with flavor, featuring tangy cheddar cheese and savory Italian sausage. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or as a satisfying snack, these biscuits are sure to impress. Quick and easy to make, they're perfect for busy mornings or lazy weekends.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups All-purpose flour
1 tbsp Baking powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 cup Unsalted butter, cold and cubed
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 cup Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
3/4 cup Milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold, cubed butter to the flour mixture and use a pastry cutter or fork to cut it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and cooked Italian sausage until evenly distributed.
  5. Add the milk to the bowl and stir until a soft dough forms.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently a few times until it comes together.
  7. Pat the dough into a circle about 1 inch (2.5 cm) thick. Use a biscuit cutter or a glass to cut out biscuits, re-rolling the scraps as needed.
  8. Place the biscuits on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Remove from the oven and serve warm. Enjoy!

Italian Sausage Biscuits

Rating: 4.5

Timing: 40 minutes

Serves: 4 - 8

Difficulty: Intermediate

Chef's Comments:

These Italian Sausage Biscuits are a flavorful twist on traditional biscuits. The combination of tangy cheddar cheese and savory Italian sausage makes them irresistible. Whether served for breakfast or as a snack, they're sure to be a hit with everyone at the table!
