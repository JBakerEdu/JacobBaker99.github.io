Recipe: Fish Sushi

This delicious Fish Sushi recipe features diced shrimp sautéed in minced buttery garlic, along with guacamole and cucumber, wrapped in sushi rice and seaweed. It's a delightful twist on traditional sushi, offering a fusion of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups Sushi rice
1/2 pound Diced shrimp
2 tbsp Butter
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1 Cucumber, thinly sliced
1 cup Guacamole
4 sheets Seaweed
To taste Soy sauce, for dipping

Instructions

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add diced shrimp to the pan and cook until they turn pink and opaque.
  4. Place a sheet of seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat.
  5. Spread a layer of sushi rice evenly over the seaweed, leaving about an inch of space at the top.
  6. Arrange sliced cucumber and cooked shrimp along the center of the rice.
  7. Top with a layer of guacamole.
  8. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, pressing gently to seal the edge.
  9. Cut the sushi roll into slices using a sharp knife.
  10. Serve with soy sauce for dipping and enjoy!

Fish Sushi

Rating: 4.7

Timing: 45 minutes

Serves: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Chefs Comments:

This Fish Sushi recipe is a personal favorite of mine. The combination of sautéed shrimp, buttery garlic, guacamole, and crunchy cucumber creates a symphony of flavors and textures that are simply irresistible. It's perfect for a special occasion or whenever you're craving something unique and delicious.
