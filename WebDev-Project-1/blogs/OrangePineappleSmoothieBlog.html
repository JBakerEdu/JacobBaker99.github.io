Recipe: Orange Pineapple Smoothie

Indulge in the refreshing taste of Orange Pineapple Smoothie! This vibrant drink is a delightful blend of tangy mandarin oranges, sweet pineapple, creamy yogurt, and honey, all brought together with a splash of vanilla soy milk. Perfect for a quick breakfast or a refreshing snack, this smoothie is sure to brighten your day.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 (11-ounce) can Mandarin oranges in juice, drained and frozen
1/2 cup Pineapple chunks (frozen)
1/2 cup Vanilla yogurt
1 tablespoon Honey
1 cup Vanilla soy milk

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Open the can of mandarin oranges and drain them.
  3. Place the drained mandarin oranges and pineapple chunks in a zip-top plastic bag and freeze them for several hours.
  4. In the blender or food processor, add the frozen mandarin oranges and pineapple chunks.
  5. Add the vanilla yogurt, honey, and vanilla soy milk in the blender.
  6. Puree the mixture until it reaches the texture of a milkshake. Add more ice if desired to achieve an icy consistency.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Orange Pineapple Smoothie

Rating: 3.7

Timing: 10 minutes

Serves: 1 - 2

Difficulty: Easy

Chef's Comments:

This Orange Pineapple Smoothie is a refreshing treat that's perfect for any time of day. With its tropical flavors and creamy texture, it's sure to become a favorite in your smoothie repertoire. Enjoy it as a quick breakfast option or as a refreshing snack on a hot day!
