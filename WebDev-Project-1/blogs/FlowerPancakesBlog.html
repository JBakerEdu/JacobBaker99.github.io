Recipe: Flowery Pancakes

Delight your taste buds with these delightful flowery pancakes. Made with love and creativity, these pancakes are not only delicious but also visually appealing. Perfect for a special breakfast or brunch, these pancakes are sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cup All-purpose flour
2 tsp Baking powder
2 tbsp Sugar
1 Egg
1 cup Milk
2 tbsp Butter, melted
1 tsp Vanilla extract
To taste Food coloring (various colors)
Vegetable oil (for cooking)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Divide the batter into separate bowls and add a few drops of food coloring to each bowl, creating different colors.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with vegetable oil.
  6. Using a ladle or measuring cup, pour small amounts of batter onto the skillet to form flower shapes.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter, adjusting the color of the pancakes as desired.
  9. Serve the flowery pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Flowery Pancakes

Rating: 4.6

Timing: 20 minutes

Serves: 3

Difficulty: Easy

Chefs Comments:

These flowery pancakes are not only delicious but also fun to make. Kids and adults alike will enjoy creating different colored pancakes and arranging them into beautiful flower patterns. Serve them for a special occasion or just to brighten up your morning!
