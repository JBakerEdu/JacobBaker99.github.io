Recipe: Stuffed Chicken Parm

This delightful Stuffed Chicken Parmesan recipe features tender chicken breasts filled with creamy fresh mozzarella cheese, coated with Italian breadcrumbs, and fried to golden perfection. Topped with marinara sauce and a generous layer of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, this dish promises to satisfy your cravings for comfort food with its rich and savory flavors.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
4 Chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
8 oz Fresh Mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup Italian breadcrumbs
1/4 cup Marinara sauce
1 Egg, beaten
To taste Salt and pepper
2 cups Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oil to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut each chicken breast to create a pocket.
  3. Stuff each pocket with fresh mozzarella cheese.
  4. Dip the stuffed chicken breasts in beaten egg.
  5. Coat the chicken breasts with breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry the breaded chicken breasts in hot oil until fully golden brown.
  7. Check the internal temperature to ensure they're cooked.
  8. Once done, remove the chicken from the oil.
  9. Serve the chicken on top of noodles.
  10. Pour sauce on top of the chicken.
  11. Optionally, add cheese according to preference.
  12. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.

Stuffed Chicken Parm

Rating: 4.8

Timing: 30 minutes

Serves: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Chefs Comments:

I wanted to redefine the classic Chicken Parmesan by stuffing it with fresh mozzarella. The result? A creamy, cheesy explosion in every bite. While noodles complement it well, this dish stands strong as tender chicken alone. With some tweaks to the breading, it's a perfect foundation for a delicious meal. And don't worry, frying is easier than you think!
