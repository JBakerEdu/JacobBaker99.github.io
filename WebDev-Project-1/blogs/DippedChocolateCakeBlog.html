Recipe: Chocolate Peanut Dipped Cake

Indulge in the decadent delight of Chocolate Peanut Dipped Cake! This heavenly creation features three layers of moist chocolate cake with a luscious peanut butter crème filling sandwiched between them. The exterior is coated in a rich dark chocolate shell, with any leftover chocolate drizzled on top for an extra indulgent touch. Chopped peanuts adorn the sides of the cake, adding a delightful crunch, while a half Reese's cup crowns the top, nestled in a dollop of whipped cream. To enhance the chocolate coating, the cake is cut into triangular slices before dipping. For convenience, a pre-mixed chocolate cake box was used to streamline the baking process.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 box Chocolate cake mix (pre-mixed)
1 jar Peanut butter crème filling
1 lb Dark chocolate
1 cup Chopped peanuts
1 Reese's cup
1 can Cool Whip

Instructions

  1. Bake the chocolate cake according to the instructions on the box and allow it to cool completely.
  2. Prepare the peanut butter crème filling and spread it evenly between the cooled cake layers.
  3. Melt the dark chocolate in a double boiler or microwave, stirring until smooth.
  4. Cut the cake into triangular slices for easier dipping.
  5. Dip each slice of cake into the melted chocolate, ensuring it is completely coated, and place it on a wire rack to set.
  6. While the chocolate is still tacky, press chopped peanuts onto the sides of each cake slice.
  7. Drizzle any leftover chocolate over the top of the cake slices.
  8. Before serving, top each cake slice with a dollop of Cool Whip and half a Reese's cup.
  9. Enjoy the indulgent combination of chocolate, peanut butter, and cake!

Chocolate Peanut Dipped Cake

Rating: 4.7

Timing: 60 minutes

Serves: 6

Difficulty: Hard

Chef's Comments:

This Chocolate Peanut Dipped Cake is a true delight for chocolate and peanut butter lovers alike. The combination of moist chocolate cake, creamy peanut butter filling, and crunchy peanuts creates a symphony of flavors and textures that's simply irresistible. Whether enjoyed as a dessert or a special treat, it's sure to be a hit at any gathering!"
