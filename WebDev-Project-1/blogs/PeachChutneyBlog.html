Recipe: Peach Chutney

Delight your senses with the exquisite flavors of homemade Peach Chutney. This delectable condiment is crafted from a unique blend of homemade vinegar-based BBQ sauce and luscious peach preserves, simmered to perfection. The result is a sweet and tangy chutney that pairs perfectly with a variety of dishes. Simply add the homemade BBQ sauce to half a jar of peach preserves and simmer until the flavors meld together harmoniously.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 jar (about 16 oz) Peach preserves
1 cup Ketchup
1/4 cup Apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons Brown sugar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon Smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder
To taste Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the homemade vinegar-based BBQ sauce. (See instructions below.)
  2. Add the homemade BBQ sauce to half a jar of peach preserves.
  3. Simmer the mixture until the flavors are well combined and the chutney reaches the desired consistency.
  4. Allow the chutney to cool before serving.
  5. Enjoy the sweet and tangy flavors of homemade Peach Chutney with your favorite dishes!

Homemade Vinegar-Based BBQ Sauce

Create your own tangy BBQ sauce with this simple recipe:

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened slightly. Allow to cool before using in the Peach Chutney recipe.

Peach Chutney

Rating: 4.2

Timing: 30 minutes

Serves: N/A

Difficulty: Easy

Chef's Comments:

This Peach Chutney is a delightful addition to any meal. The combination of sweet peach preserves and tangy BBQ sauce creates a unique flavor profile that elevates both savory and sweet dishes. Whether served as a condiment or used as a glaze, it's sure to impress!
